Privacy Policy

At Ovarzaki, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account in the App, we may collect information such as your name, email address, and login credentials.
  • App Usage Information: We may collect information about how you use the App, such as the features you use and the time you spend in the App.
  • Information from Facebook and Instagram (with your consent): With your explicit consent, we may collect information from your Facebook and Instagram accounts, solely for the purposes described below.

Permissions We Request and How We Use Them

We request the following permissions from Facebook and Instagram to enhance your App experience and provide our services effectively, in accordance with Meta's usage guidelines:

  • pages_read_engagement: To read data about user engagement with the Pages you manage, for analyzing content performance and providing recommendations for improvement.
  • pages_manage_ads: To manage ads for the Pages you manage, such as creating, editing, and pausing ad campaigns.
  • ads_management: To manage your Facebook ad accounts.
  • ads_read: To read ad performance data.
  • business_management: To manage business assets, such as Pages and ad accounts.
  • pages_manage_metadata: To manage Page metadata, such as changing the profile picture and Page information.
  • publish_video: To publish videos to the Pages you manage.
  • pages_user_gender: To access the gender of users who interact with the Pages, for demographic analysis purposes.
  • instagram_business_manage_messages: To manage messages sent to Instagram business accounts.
  • Business asset user profile access: To access user profile information within business assets.
  • Ads Management Standard Access: To access core ad management functionalities.

Important Notes Regarding Permission Usage

  • We only request permissions that are necessary to provide our services.
  • We use these permissions solely for the purposes described above, and we do not share this data with third parties without your consent or as required by law.
  • We adhere to Meta's usage guidelines for the aforementioned permissions.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving the App's services.
  • Communicating with you about your account and important updates.
  • Analyzing App usage to improve user experience.
  • Providing technical support.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • With your explicit consent.
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations.
  • To protect our rights or the rights of others.

Security of Your Information

We take reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
